## Overview

The **Baalvion Unified Dashboard** brings the operations of the entire group into a single console —
people, performance, and analytics across every business, with role-aware access powered by the
platform's centralized identity.

## ✨ Features

- **Operations overview** — cross-business KPIs and live metrics
- **People & HR** — teams, attendance, and workforce insights
- **Analytics & forecasting** — trends and reporting in one place
- **Role-aware** — access scoped by the platform RBAC hierarchy
